Day 1: Monuments and the National Mall


Morning:
1. Start your day with a visit to the Washington Monument. You can take an elevator to the top for stunning views of the city.
2. Walk down the National Mall, stopping at the World War II Memorial and the Korean War Veterans Memorial.
3. Continue to the Lincoln Memorial, with its impressive statue of Abraham Lincoln and views of the Reflecting Pool.

Lunch:
4. Have lunch at one of the food trucks or nearby restaurants around the National Mall.

Afternoon:
5. Explore the Vietnam Veterans Memorial and the Martin Luther King Jr. Memorial.

Day 2: Museums and Capitol Hill


Morning:
1.Spend your morning at the Smithsonian National Air and Space Museum, where you can marvel at historic aircraft and spacecraft.
Lunch:
2. Enjoy lunch at one of the museum's cafeterias or a nearby restaurant.

Afternoon:
3. Visit the United States Capitol. You can take a guided tour of the Capitol Building or explore the exterior and the Capitol Visitor Center.

4. Explore the Library of Congress, home to a vast collection of books and historic documents.
5. Visit the Supreme Court of the United States.
Dinner:
6. Dine in the historic Capitol Hill neighborhood.

Day 3: The White House, Museums, and Georgetown


Morning:
1. Start your day at the White House Visitor Center, where you can learn about the history and significance of the White House.
2. Take a short walk to Lafayette Square for photos of the White House from the outside.
Lunch:
3. Enjoy lunch at a restaurant in the vicinity.

Afternoon:
4. Explore the National Gallery of Art, with its impressive collection of artwork.
5. Visit the National Museum of American History, showcasing the country's history and culture.
6. Stroll through the vibrant Georgetown neighborhood, exploring its charming streets, shops, and waterfront.

Dinner:
7. End your trip with a meal at one of Georgetown's renowned restaurants or waterfront eateries.
